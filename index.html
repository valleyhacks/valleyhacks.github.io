Welcome!

VALLEYhacks is a student-run hackathon targeted and created for high school students in the Bay Area.

What is VALLEYhacks?

VALLEYhacks aims to be the most social high-school hackathon in the Bay Area. This means 24 straight hours full of workshops, activities, mentors, icebreakers, and most importantly: free swag.

I don't know how to code, should I come?

Of course! Hackathons are an amazing experience for seasoned coders and first time coders alike! We'll have plenty of workshops and mentors going around to help you with whatever project you want to create!

Cool! How much does it cost?

It's free! With our generous sponsors handling the fiscal side of things, you're able to spend your 24-hours purely immersed into your own coding world!

Should you apply?

VALLEYhacks aims to be the most social high-school hackathon in the Bay Area. This means 24 straight hours full of workshops, activities, mentors, icebreakers, and most importantly: free swag.

As a team, we want students from all backgrounds to spend 24 hours learning and sharing their knowledge about the computer-science field about everything from blockchain to AI to an app that cleans water bottles; in short, we want everyone to innovate and pioneer while networking to meet new people from all creeds.

Being a part of a hackathon should be on every high school student's list and we highly encourage it!

tl;dr: The best 24 hours you'll have all summer.

CULTURE

As strong believers that teens are the future of this nation, we want to spread our passion of computer science and engineering to everyone and anyone that is looking to get into the field. With the help of our generous sponsors and the help of local orgainzations, we have been able to turn our dream into a reality and educate students on global benefits that code can provide.

These 24-hours will be packed from all types of opportunities from coding workshops to wide-scale interpersonal skill workshops that help build communication and presentation skills.
