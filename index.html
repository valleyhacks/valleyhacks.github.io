FAQ

What if I can't code?

That's not an issue at all! Many hackathon attendees are first-time coders who learn from their peers and the numerous workshops we'll be hosting throughout the hackathon! All levels of coding knowledge, beginner to PhD, are encouraged to come and network with fellow hackers as well as build their own skillsets!

How much does it cost?

Zero, nada, and nilch. Our hackathon is 100% free for attendees. Just bring your equipment and we'll provide you with the necessary electricity and fuel to get you through the event!

Where is VALLEYhacks?

For our first event, we are glad to be partnered with Mozilla and we will host our hackathon in their Mountain View Headquarters on the weekend of June 23-24, 2018.

Who can participate?

VALLEYhacks is a high-school-only hackathon. Any high school student looking to compete will be able to. If you are a college student looking to help out, feel free to apply as a mentor!

What should I bring?

All attendees should bring a valid form of ID. Sleeping bags and other toiletries are recommended.

Will there be prizes?

Of Course! At the end of the 24-hour hacking period when groups submit their projects, a panel of judges will decide which projects receive an awesome reward!
