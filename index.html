VALLEYhacks is a student-run hackathon targeted and created for high school students in the Bay Area.

What is VALLEYhacks?

VALLEYhacks aims to be the most social high-school hackathon in the Bay Area. This means 24 straight hours full of workshops, activities, mentors, icebreakers, and most importantly: free swag.

I don't know how to code, should I come?

Of course! Hackathons are an amazing experience for seasoned coders and first time coders alike! We'll have plenty of workshops and mentors going around to help you with whatever project you want to create!

Cool! How much does it cost?

It's free! With our generous sponsors handling the fiscal side of things, you're able to spend your 24-hours purely immersed into your own coding world!

FAQ

Where is VALLEYhacks?

For our first event, we are glad to be partnered with Mozilla and we will host our hackathon in their Mountain View Headquarters on the weekend of June 23-24, 2018.

Who can participate?

VALLEYhacks is a high-school-only hackathon. Any high school student looking to compete will be able to. If you are a college student looking to help out, feel free to apply as a mentor!

What should I bring?

All attendees should bring a valid form of ID. Sleeping bags and other toiletries are recommended. Please don't forget your laptop and your charger!

Will there be prizes?

Of Course! At the end of the 24-hour hacking period when groups submit their projects, a panel of judges will decide which projects receive an awesome reward!
